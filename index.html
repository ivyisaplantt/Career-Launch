Budget Tracker for Groceries



How it works:

Use our product search to look up any product between five grocery stores in the College Park area. Then, use the price comparison tool to find the best price for your groceries.



Budgeting Tips:
  • Try to plan out what you buy first and buy in bulk.
  • Use various store loyalty programs to get access to weekly deals and various discounts.
  • UIDs can often get you 5-10% off at various grocery stores.
  • Shop during the morning and/or evening to receive discounts on items such as baked goods and meats.
  • Buy the generic brands of foods, even though they're cheaper than name brand, they're often cheaper.
